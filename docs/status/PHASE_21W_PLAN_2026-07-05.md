## Activation-Grade Policy

Activation-grade sensitivity requires all of:

- `full_default_grid_complete=true`
- `completion_status=COMPLETE`
- deterministic `grid_version` and `grid_hash`
- no partial-disclosure warning
- no failed required scenario except as explicit robustness failure
- `pass_fail=pass` for promotion-sensitive review

Diagnostic sensitivity can be `TIERED_ESSENTIAL`, `SAMPLED`, `PARTIAL_TIMEOUT`, or incomplete `CHUNKED_FULL_GRID`, but it cannot satisfy required activation-grade sensitivity.
