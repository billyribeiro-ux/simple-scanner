# Roadmap

## V1

- FMP historical bars and quote ingestion.
- Feature and label generation.
- Baseline rules/statistical/ML ensemble.
- Walk-forward validation.
- REST polling live scanner.
- Dashboard, CSV/XLSX exports, and daily review scaffold.

## V2

- Additional provider adapters for options, market internals, order book, news/catalyst scoring, sector rotation, and richer ETF context.
- Paper-trading simulator.
- More advanced online learning with drift monitoring.
- Broker integrations only after explicit approval.
